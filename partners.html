A List of our Partners

We are proud to share some of the best volunteering centers, shelters, and charities for animals who we have partnered with!

Partners

Best Friends NYC

A no-kill animal shelter and adoption/volunteering center located near Pace University, on 307 West Broadway, Best Friends is a highly beginner-friendly volunteer center and are well-regarded for their comprehensive animal care!

Paws NYC

Paws is a reputed animal care firm with many support programs to help older, low-income individuals and families continue to afford pet care. They also take volunteers, though they are more restrictive with the volunteer registration process. Their mission is to help ensure pets stay in their loving homes and owners aren't at risk of losing them due to financial or physical difficulties.
