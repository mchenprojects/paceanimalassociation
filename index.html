Who We Are

Welcome to Pace Animal Association! We're a club at Pace University dedicated to creating a community for like-minded animal lovers and a platform for advocacy regarding animals! If you're looking for a club where you can bond with people over a shared love for pets, volunteering for animals, or donating to endangered animal causes, then consider attending one of our events. We aim to connect students to animal volunteering centers, shelters, and charities, all while sharing a few cat or dog memes along the way!

We are in the process of filing for 501(c)(3) status as a nonprofit, and renovate our platform to include a donations feature directly to our partnered charities! Stay tuned!

Past Events

Dog Toys from Old Clothes

Members brought in old articles of clothing to learn to turn them into dog toys, which were then donated to numerous charities and shelters. We hosted a mini arts and crafts workshop with some materials being provided to learn to disassemble and repurpose apparel into toys.

Sea Turtles Fundraiser

Members helped create goodie bags to be sold during a fundraiser dedicated to raising funds for endangered sea turtle species. Goodie bags consisted of pins, plushies, and snacks; a total of $400 was generated to donate to a predetermined charity.
